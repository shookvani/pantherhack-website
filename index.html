About PantherHack

We are Princeton Day School’s first-ever hackathon! This virtual event will take place this coming April for 24 hours, and it will be open to high school students across the U.S for a thrilling experience full of collaboration and technological innovation.

In order to guide our participants, we will release various challenges throughout the competition that will test students’ abilities to think outside the box and implement creative solutions to real-world problems.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?
A Hackathon is an event where students passionate about STEM come together to create a “hack” in teams. This “hack” could be anything that is created from scratch between the time slot provided using a computer or technology. At the end of the 4 days, the best “hack” is chosen to be the winner of HackJPS!

Who can attend?
All high school and middle school students with an interest in STEM are welcome to attend!

How much does it cost?
The event is completely free to attend! Our amazing sponsors help us cover the cost to run HackJPS. You have nothing to lose if you attend.

When is it?
The event will run from September 18th - 21st. The exact timings will be finalized shortly.

How will an online hackathon work?
It will proceed very similarly to a normal hackathon, just with all communication occurring online. Through Youtube live streams, Discord, and many other platforms, we hope to bridge the gap between everyone and create a vibrant atmosphere.

Do I need coding/hackathon experience to attend?
Everyone is welcome to participate, regardless of their coding experience. There will be many mentors and workshops to guide you along the way!

What will I need?
A laptop, chargers, any hardware for your project, stable wifi connection, and most importantly an open mind to learn new things!

Do I need a team before I can sign up?
If you don’t have a team before the event, don’t worry! We encourage you to meet new students during or at the beginning of the event through Discord. Teams of 4 are recommended.

What can I win?
Prizes range from $25 to $600. Regardless, you’ll be able to meet new friends, find new contacts, and create unique projects. The experience you gain is much more valuable than any monetary prize.
